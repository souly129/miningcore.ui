Getting Started with Firo Mining

Learn step-by-step how to mine Firo efficiently and connect to the Meowpool for optimal rewards using your CPU or GPU.

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

Before mining, you need a Firo wallet to receive your payouts:

  • Download the official Firo wallet from firo.org.
  • Create a new wallet address dedicated to mining rewards.
  • Back up your wallet and recovery phrase securely.
  • Ensure your wallet is fully synced with the Firo network.
Tip: Use a dedicated mining wallet to keep funds separate from personal transactions.

Step 2: Choose Your Miner

For Firo, the recommended miner is SRBMiner. Make sure to download the latest version compatible with your OS.

Tip: Verify the miner binary checksums to avoid malware.

Step 3: Connect to Meowpool

Set up your miner to connect to Meowpool using the following stratum URL:

stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:7278

Use your wallet address as the worker name. You can leave the password as “x” or any value.

Example SRBMiner command:

SRBMiner -a MTP -o stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:7278 -u YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS -p x

Step 4: Start Mining

Run your miner and monitor your hashrate. You can track your shares and earnings on the Meowpool dashboard.

  • Check for rejected shares — a small number is normal; many indicate connection or configuration issues.
  • Adjust miner intensity or CPU/GPU settings for optimal efficiency and temperature management.
  • Regularly monitor your hardware to prevent overheating.

Step 5: Troubleshooting

  • Miner not connecting: Ensure your firewall allows outbound TCP to port 7278.
  • Low hashrate: Verify you are using the correct miner version and drivers.
  • High rejected shares: Try lowering intensity or changing pool settings.
  • Pool dashboard not updating: Wait a few minutes and check that your wallet address is correct.

Step 6: Next Steps & Tips

  • Experiment with miner settings for the best performance vs stability.
  • Keep your wallet and miner software updated for security.
  • Check profitability periodically as network difficulty and coin price change.
  • Join the Meowpool community for tips, updates, and support.
