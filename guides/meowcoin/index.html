Getting Started with Meowcoin Mining

Follow this detailed guide to mine Meowcoin using the official pool and supported miners. We’ll cover wallet setup, miner selection, pool connection, and optimization tips.

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

  • Download the official Meowcoin wallet from meowcoin.
  • Install it on your computer and create a new wallet address dedicated to mining.
  • Securely back up your wallet and recovery phrase.
  • Ensure your wallet is fully synced with the network before mining.
Tip: Keep your mining wallet separate from personal transactions to simplify tracking rewards.

Step 2: Choose Your Miner

Recommended miners for Meowcoin:

  • MeowPowMiner – Optimized for Meowcoin’s algorithm, supports GPU mining.
  • SRBMiner-Multi – Flexible miner supporting multiple coins and devices.
  • Download the latest version compatible with your OS and verify checksums to avoid malware.
Tip: Running multiple GPUs? SRBMiner-Multi is ideal for managing all devices from one instance.

Step 3: Connect to Meowpool

Use the following pool address and configure your miner:

stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3094

Use your wallet address as the worker name. Password can be left as “x”.

Example command for MeowPowMiner:

MeowPowMiner.exe -o stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3094 -u YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS -p x

Step 4: Start Mining

Run your miner and monitor your hashrate and shares. Track your earnings on the Meowpool dashboard.

  • Check for rejected shares — a few are normal; high numbers indicate configuration issues.
  • Adjust miner settings to maintain hardware stability and optimal performance.
  • Monitor GPU/CPU temperatures to prevent overheating.

Step 5: Troubleshooting

  • Miner not connecting: Ensure TCP outbound to port 3094 is allowed by your firewall.
  • Low hashrate: Verify miner version, drivers, and GPU/CPU utilization.
  • High rejected shares: Try lowering intensity or switching stratum server.
  • Dashboard not updating: Mining shares can take a few minutes to appear; double-check your wallet address.

Step 6: Next Steps & Tips

  • Experiment with different miner settings for performance vs stability.
  • Keep your wallet and miner software updated for security.
  • Regularly check profitability as network difficulty and coin price change.
  • Join the Meowpool community for news, updates, and support.
