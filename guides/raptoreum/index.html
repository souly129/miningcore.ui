Getting Started with Raptoreum Mining

This guide will take you step-by-step through mining Raptoreum using CPU or GPU miners, connecting to Meowpool, and optimizing your setup.

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

  • Download the official Raptoreum wallet from raptoreum.com.
  • Install it on your computer and create a new wallet address dedicated for mining rewards.
  • Backup your wallet and recovery phrase securely.
  • Ensure the wallet is fully synced with the network.
Tip: Use a separate wallet for mining to keep earnings organized and secure.

Step 2: Choose Your Miner

Recommended miners for Raptoreum:

  • XMRig – Supports CPU mining efficiently and is widely used in the community.
  • Ensure you download the correct version for your operating system.
  • Verify the binary checksum/signature to avoid malware.
Tip: CPU mining is the most effective for Raptoreum; GPU mining is possible but less common.

Step 3: Connect to Meowpool

Configure your miner with the following pool details:

stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3032

Use your wallet address as the worker name. Password can be set to "x".

Example XMRig command:

xmrig -o stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3032 -u YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS -p x

Step 4: Start Mining

Run your miner and monitor your hashrate and shares. Track your mining rewards on the Meowpool dashboard.

  • Check rejected shares — a few are normal; high numbers indicate connection or config issues.
  • Adjust miner settings to optimize performance while keeping hardware safe.
  • Monitor CPU/GPU temperatures and utilization to prevent overheating.

Step 5: Troubleshooting

  • Miner not connecting: Check firewall and outbound TCP port 3032.
  • Low hashrate: Verify miner version and system performance.
  • High rejected shares: Reduce intensity or try alternative stratum server if available.
  • Dashboard not updating: Mining shares may take a few minutes to appear; ensure wallet address is correct.

Step 6: Next Steps & Tips

  • Experiment with different miner settings for optimal performance and stability.
  • Keep your wallet and miner software updated for security.
  • Regularly check Raptoreum network difficulty and coin profitability.
  • Join the Meowpool community for updates, tips, and announcements.
