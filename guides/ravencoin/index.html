Getting Started with Ravencoin Mining

This guide will walk you through mining Ravencoin using GPU miners, connecting to Meowpool, and optimizing your setup for best performance.

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

  • Download the official Ravencoin wallet from ravencoin.org.
  • Install it and create a new wallet address dedicated for mining rewards.
  • Backup your wallet and recovery phrase securely.
  • Ensure your wallet is fully synced with the network before mining.
Tip: Use a dedicated wallet for mining to avoid mixing funds with personal transactions.

Step 2: Choose Your Miner

Recommended GPU miners for Ravencoin:

  • T-Rex Miner – Efficient for NVIDIA GPUs and widely used.
  • SRBMiner-Multi – Supports AMD and NVIDIA GPUs, stable for long runs.
  • Download the miner for your operating system and verify its checksum.
Tip: Keep your GPU drivers up to date for optimal performance.

Step 3: Connect to Meowpool

Configure your miner with the following pool details:

stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3094

Use your wallet address as the worker name. Password can be "x".

Example T-Rex command:

t-rex -a kawpow -o stratum+tcp://meowpool.net:3094 -u YOUR_WALLET_ADDRESS -p x

Step 4: Start Mining

Run your miner and monitor your hashrate. Track your mining rewards on the Meowpool dashboard.

  • Check for rejected shares — a few are normal; high numbers indicate config issues.
  • Adjust GPU settings such as power limit and fan speed to maintain efficiency and prevent overheating.
  • Monitor performance regularly to avoid hardware damage.

Step 5: Troubleshooting

  • Miner not connecting: Ensure your firewall allows outbound TCP to port 3094.
  • Low hashrate: Verify miner version and GPU settings.
  • High rejected shares: Try lowering intensity or switching to an alternate stratum server if available.
  • Dashboard not updating: Mining shares may take a few minutes to appear; double-check your wallet address.

Step 6: Next Steps & Tips

  • Experiment with different miner settings to find the best balance between performance and stability.
  • Regularly update your miner software and wallet for security.
  • Check Ravencoin network difficulty and coin profitability periodically.
  • Join the Meowpool community for updates, tips, and announcements.
